Common Questions

How long does a typical project take? +
A standard landing page takes 3-7 days. More complex business sites or e-commerce stores typically take 2-3 weeks depending on the scope.

I offer unlimited revisions during the design phase to ensure you are 100% happy with the look and feel before we move to development.
What are your payment terms? +
I usually require a 50% upfront deposit to secure your slot in my calendar, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion and your final approval.

Yes, I provide 30 days of free support after launch. I also offer monthly maintenance packages for updates, security, and performance monitoring.
