Got Questions?

FAQ

How long does a website take to build? +
Most projects are completed within 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. A landing page can be done in 3–5 days, while a full e-commerce store may take 2–4 weeks.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes! Every project includes up to 3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're 100% happy with the result before we go live.
What do you need to get started? +
I'll need your brand assets (logo, colors, fonts), content (text and images), and a clear brief of what you're looking for. Don't have all of this? No problem — I can help with content strategy too.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance? +
Absolutely. I offer monthly maintenance packages that cover updates, backups, security, and minor content changes.
What's your payment process? +
I require a 50% deposit upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion before final delivery.
