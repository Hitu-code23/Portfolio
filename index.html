FAQ

Things You
Should Know

We've answered the most common questions to help you get started with clarity.

How do I start searching for a home?
You can browse available properties, apply filters, and explore listings based on your needs and location.
Can I filter homes by budget & location?
Yes, you can easily filter properties by price range, location, and other preferences to find exactly what you're looking for.
Are the property details accurate?
We provide updated and verified details to help you make informed decisions. All listings are reviewed before publishing.
How do I contact a owner or agent?
Each listing includes contact options so you can directly connect with the owner or agent for more information.
Can I explore rental and buying options
Yes, you can browse both rental and purchase options in one place using the filter toggles.
Do I need an account to use platform
You can browse freely, but creating an account helps you save and manage your preferences and listings.
How do I know if a property is available?
Availability is updated regularly, but we recommend contacting the listing directly for real-time confirmation.
